About Us

The Mugabel family owned a modest used car lot in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, dealing primarily in reliable and affordable vehicles. Over the years, their business managed to carve out a niche in a market that appreciated value and practicality. However, as the automotive market evolved and competition increased, the family recognized a need to diversify their business. In 2021, Ibraheem Mugabel, the eldest son, relocated to the U.S. with an idea: to establish a similar used car dealership in Seattle, focusing on affordable imported cars and providing value to budget-conscious consumers. His father, Hashem, provided financial backing, while his younger brother, AlBaraa, continued to manage the family's original lot in Riyadh.
