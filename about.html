About Us

The Mugabel family owned a modest used car lot in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, dealing primarily in reliable and affordable vehicles.
Over the years, their business managed to carve out a niche in a market that appreciated value and practicality.
However, as the automotive market evolved and competition increased, the family recognized a need to diversify their business.
In 2021, Ibraheem Mugabel, the eldest son, relocaed to the U.S. with an idea: to establish a similar used car dealership in
Seattle, focusing on affordable imported cars and providing value to budget-conscious consumers. His father, Hashem, provided
financial backing, while his younger brother, AlBaraa, continued to manage the family's original lot in Riyadh.
Starting a business in the U.S. posed significant challenges for Ibraheem. He encountered strict regulations, licensing
requirements, and a completely different market landscape. Initially, he took a job at a local dealership in Seattle to
learn about the American automotive market. After gaining two years of experience, he opened "Fadak Auto", a dealership
that specialized in offering a mix of used imported and domestic vehicles that appealed to a wide range of buyers in Seattle.
Word of the dealership's value-driven approach spread, attracting a diverse clientele including immigrants, students, and
local residents looking for good deals. Back in Riyadh, Albaraa adapted the family business to better utilize digital platforms,
expanding their visibility and customer base throughout the region.

Despite the physical distance, the Mugabel brothers maintained the entrepreneurial spirit of their family. Their dealerships
in Riyadh and Seattle exemplified their ability to adapt and thrive in different markets through a shared commitment to their
community and customers.
