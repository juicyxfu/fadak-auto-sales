About Us

The Mugabel family owned a modest used car lot in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, dealing primarily in reliable and affordable vehicles. Over the years, their business carved out a niche in a market that appreciated value and practicality.

In 2021, Ibraheem Mugabel, the eldest son, relocated to the U.S. with an idea: to establish a similar used car dealership in Seattle, focusing on affordable imported cars and providing value to budget-conscious consumers. His father, Hashem, provided financial backing, while his younger brother, AlBaraa, continued to manage the family's original lot in Riyadh.

After gaining two years of experience in Seattle, Ibraheem opened **Fadak Auto**, a dealership specializing in affordable, high-quality vehicles. Meanwhile, Albaraa expanded the family business online, reaching customers across the region.

Despite the physical distance, the Mugabel brothers upheld their family's entrepreneurial spirit, growing Fadak Auto into a trusted name in both the U.S. and Saudi Arabia.
